Who We Are

ByteSpector is an advanced forensic and cryptography toolkit built for cybersecurity enthusiasts, ethical hackers, and students. It provides an interactive environment to analyze files, detect hidden information, visualize steganographic data, and uncover unseen patterns in digital artifacts.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make digital forensics and cryptanalysis more accessible and intuitive. We combine simplicity with power — whether you're decoding file headers, examining image metadata, or visualizing least significant bits, ByteSpector gives you the clarity and control you need.
